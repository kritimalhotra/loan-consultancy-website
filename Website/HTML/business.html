Eligibility Criteria

  1. Age Requirement: The applicant must be between 21 and 65 years of age.
  2. Business Vintage: Minimum 2 years of operational history.
  3. Minimum Turnover: Annual turnover of at least ₹10 lakhs.
  4. Profitability: Business should be profitable for the last 2 financial years.
  1. Credit Score: Preferred minimum CIBIL score of 650.
  2. Business Registration: Legally registered with relevant authorities (GST, Shop Act, etc.).
  3. Bank Account: Active business bank account with regular transactions.
  4. Debt Repayment History: Clean repayment history with no significant defaults.
